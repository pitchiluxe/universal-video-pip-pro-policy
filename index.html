Universal Video PiP PRO – Privacy Policy

This browser extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data about you.

The extension only stores a small set of user-chosen settings (such as whether auto Picture-in-Picture on scroll or on tab switch is enabled) using the browser’s built-in chrome.storage.sync API. These settings are used solely to control the behavior of the extension and may sync between your own devices via your browser account.

The extension does not send any data to external servers, does not use analytics, and does not display ads.
